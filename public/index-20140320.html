Past Work

Google+ Local: helps users discover and share places. Integrating Zagat's scoring system and summaries of user reviews, as well as a recommendation system based on your circles, your past reviews and your locations.

Google Maps Place Pages: a webpage for every place in the world, organizing all the relevant information about it. Before Google+ Local, clicking on the "more info" link in a Google Maps location took users to the Place Page about that location.

iGoogle: a customizable Ajax-based startpage or personal web portal, providing the ability to add web feeds and small Javascript gadgets to a "Google Personalized Homepage". iGoogle was launched in May 2005, and will be discontinued in November 2013.

ERIC: an embodied real-time intelligent commentary agent (and framework): the website, and the demo video.

2004/2005 NICTA Taste Of Research scholarship: the website, report, the poster, and the demonstration (currently not working).
